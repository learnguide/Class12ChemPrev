Define the following with an example of each :
(a)
Polysaccharides
(b)
Denatured protein
(c)
Essential amino acids
Ans.
(a)
Polysaccharides : These are the saccharides containing large number monosaccharides units joined
together by glycosidic linkage. eg:- starch
(b)
Denatured protein : Denatured protein are the protein is when subjected to physical change like change
in temperature or chemical change like pH, the hydrogen bonds are disturbed and loses its biological
activity. eg:- The coagulation of egg white on boiling.
(c)
Essential amino acids : The amino acids which cannot be synthesised in the body and must be obtained
from diet are known as essential amino acids.eg:- Histidine, Valine etc.
