Give reasons for the following :
(a)
Measurement of osmotic pressure method is preferred for the determination of molar masses of
macromolecules such as proteins and polymers.
Ans.
The osmotic pressure method has the advantage over other method as pressure measurement is around the
room temperature and the molarity of the solution is used instead of molality. Also as compared to other
colligative property its magnitude is large even for dilute solution.
