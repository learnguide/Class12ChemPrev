Define fuel cell and write its two advantages.
Ans.

designed to convert the energy from the combustion of fuel directly into elelectrical energy.

(1) Fuel cell works with an efficiency of 60 to 70 %
(2) These are no objectionable by products and therefore, its a pollution free source of energy.
